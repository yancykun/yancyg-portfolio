About

I am a Computer Science graduate from the University of Southern Mindanao, with a Bachelor's degree in the field. Throughout my academic career, I have honed my problem-solving skills. When I was in my third year of college, I made the decision to learn web development. So, I took the initiative to learn independently by following roadmaps, enrolling in online courses, and watching YouTube tutorials.

I dedicated more than 10 hours a day to coding and improving my skills. As a result, I have acquired a decent understanding of the powerful trio of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and have started exploring additional libraries such as Tailwind CSS and React JS.

Presently, my primary focus is on building interactive, responsive, and elegant websites for both web and mobile platforms. I maintain an unwavering curiosity and love for learning new things. I take great pride in delivering high-quality work and am committed to meeting deadlines. Despite my limited experience and skills, I approach each challenge with confidence and believe that I can make valuable contributions to any team.
